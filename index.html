Usage Information

Software As A Service(SaaS)

It is a delivery model in which a software application is hosted by a third-party provider and made available to customers over the Internet. Customers can access the software through a web browser or a mobile app, without the need to install and maintain it on their own local servers or computers.

On Premise Setup

It is a software that is installed and runs on a customer’s own local servers or computers. The customer is responsible for setting up, maintaining and upgrading the software, as well as ensuring its proper functioning and security.
